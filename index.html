Characteristics

Daddy long legs, also known as harvestmen (creepy!), are cute and fascinating creatures. Some of their defining characteristics are:

  • Legs: Their legs are up to 10 times the length of their bodies (hence the long legs name).
  • Body: Their bodies are typically round or oval.
  • Eyes: Most have only two, though large, eyes.

Habitats

Daddy long legs can be found in a variety of places:

Habitat Description
Houses Warm and safe.
Gardens Abundant plant matter to feed on.
Under rocks Provides shelter and humidity.

Fun Facts

Here are some interesting facts about daddy long legs:

  1. They are not spiders. They are Opiliones.
  2. Daddy long legs are harmless to (most) humans.
  3. They primarily feed on decaying plant material, small insects and the dreams of small children.
